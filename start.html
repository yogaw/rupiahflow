Trust & Safety

What is IDRX?

IDRX is a digital representation of the Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) that enables instant transfers and smart contract functionality while maintaining 1:1 value with the traditional IDR. It's fully backed by regulated financial institutions.

How do I withdraw?

You can convert your IDRX to traditional IDR at any time and withdraw to your bank account. Withdrawals typically process within 1 business day to any Indonesian bank account.

Is this legal?

Yes, RupiahFlow operates in compliance with Indonesian financial regulations. We work with licensed financial institutions and follow all KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) requirements.
