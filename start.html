Trust & Safety FAQ

How is my data protected?

Your data is encrypted using bank-level security standards. We comply with all Indonesian data protection regulations and never share your information with unauthorized parties.

Is RupiahFlow regulated?

Yes, RupiahFlow operates in compliance with OJK regulations for financial services and Bank Indonesia guidelines for payment systems.

What happens if my client doesn't pay?

RupiahFlow assumes the payment risk. You keep the advance even if your client is late or defaults. We handle collections professionally while preserving your client relationship.
